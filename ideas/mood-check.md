# Mood Check

> Your feelings matter. Check in with yourself, get a warm response.

## Problem

People often ignore how they're feeling until emotions build up. Without regular check-ins, they lose touch with their emotional patterns and miss opportunities to care for themselves.

## Solution

A simple daily mood check-in where users select how they're feeling and receive a compassionate, personalized AI response that acknowledges their emotions and offers gentle support.

## Emotional Hook

Feel HEARD and VALIDATED when you share how you're feeling. Whether you're having a great day or a tough one, receive a response that meets you exactly where you are with warmth and understanding.

## Target User

Anyone who wants to build emotional awareness but finds journaling too time-consuming. People who need a quick moment of validation and self-compassion in their busy day.

## Core Feature

Select your mood (emoji-based) + optional note -> receive a warm, validating AI response that acknowledges your feelings.

## Success Metric

Weekly active users who check in 3+ days per week.
